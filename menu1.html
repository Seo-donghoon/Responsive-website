Swimming pool

Night view

The Marina Bay Sands Light and Water Show is one show that you can watch in Singapore every night for free. The shows start at 8pm and 9.30pm. On Friday and Saturday nights, there is another show at 11pm.

Swimming pool

But here comes the big 'but': Pool access is reserved for guests of the hotel. So you can't just put on your swimsuit and jump in the pool. Unfortunately there are no exceptions, otherwise the pool would probably be bursting at the seams.
